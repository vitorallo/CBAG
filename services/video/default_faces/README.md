# Default avatar faces

`man.jpg` and `woman.jpg` are the fallback portraits the talking-head stage uses when
the user does **not** upload a photo (the default face follows the chosen voice:
female → `woman.jpg`, male → `man.jpg`).

## Provenance & licensing

**These are synthetic, AI-generated faces. They do not depict any real person.** They are
provided as-is for this demo:

- **No likeness / right-of-publicity issue** — there is no real person behind either face,
  so animating them to speak raises no consent or publicity concern.
- **No copyright claim** — they are purely AI-generated (no human authorship) and are
  included here free of any rights claim.

Consent in the app is therefore required **only for an uploaded (real) photo** — never for
these synthetic defaults. To appear as yourself, upload your own portrait in the web app.
